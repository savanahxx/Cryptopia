ABOUT

MULTISIG SECURITY

Your Cryptopia account is your wallet. Non-custodial with a baked-in
Multisig feature, it functions as both an in-game and dedicated stand-alone wallet.

  • On-Chain Multi Factor Authentication (2FA, MFA)
  • Multisig Wallet (One Wallet can have multiple owners, makes it safe)
  • Stores Native Cryptocurrencies (ETH), Tokens (ERC20) and NFTs (ERC721)
  • Transfer, Swap, Buy and Sell directly from your wallet

EXPERIENCE

FRICTIONLESS GAMING

The current blockchain gaming user experience is complicated,
similar to the early internet. Our integrated wallet is the solution for
large adoption:

  • Abstracts away complicated blockchain mechanics
  • Makes playing Cryptopia as easy as browsing the web in 2023
  • Doesn’t require MetaMask or other external wallets

MESH NETWORK

NODES

Peer-To-Peer player run nodes that facilitate decentralized network traffic. Active nodes are rewarded with a percentage of the game treasury. Whereas regular games 'burn' spent resources, in Cryptopia, it flows back into the treasury. In Phase two, node operators will be able to perform transactions and earn fees. Node operators may be rewarded for renting out their Cryptopia node to provide network traffic services for other projects.
